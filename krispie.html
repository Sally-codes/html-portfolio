Peanut Butter Rice Crispie Treats

These are the best rice crispie treats!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 10 cups rice cereal
  • 1 stick butter
  • 16 oz of marshmellows
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. melt butter & peanut butter in large pan on low heat.
  2. add marshmellows to pan. continue melting until most marshmellows are melted.
  3. add salt to melted marshmellows.
  4. mix the cereal in.
  5. set the rice crispie treats in a 8 x 8 pan. Let rest for at least two hours. Enjoy!

This is the best recipe in the world! Give it a try & let me know how you like it in the comments!

Storing: Store no more than two days at room temperature in airtight container. To freeze, place in layers separated by wax paper in airtight container. Freeze for up to 6 weeks. Let stand at room temperature for 15 minutes before serving.
